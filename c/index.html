Brick + Beam is a community for building rehabbers of all levels. We aim to support rehab in Detroit by sharing knowledge, building talent, and providing access to the resources you need to get (or keep!) going.

Sign up for our email newsletter to get an update when this site is launched, visit us on Facebook, or drop us a line at brickandbeamdetroit@gmail.com. #bragmyrehab
